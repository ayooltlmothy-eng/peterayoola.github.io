About Me

A Professional Built on Two Disciplines

I am Ayoola Peter Timothy, a Service Engineer currently working at Rehoboth Systems and Project Limited in Lagos State. My career journey has been uniquely shaped by two very different but complementary disciplines.

I hold a B.A. in English and Literary Studies from Ekiti State University, a foundation that sharpened my analytical thinking, professional communication, and ability to convey complex ideas with clarity and precision.

Driven by a passion for technology, I went on to develop strong electrical engineering skills — specialising in UPS, Inverter, and CCTV installation and maintenance. I also bring hands-on experience as a former Call Center Agent and NYSC Class Teacher, making me a well-rounded professional capable of excelling in technical and people-facing roles alike.

I am also a Certified Personnel Manager (CIHRM) and hold certifications in Human Resources, Project Management, and Health, Safety, Security & Environment (HSSE).
